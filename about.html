About Rohtak Taxi Service

Your trusted travel partner since 2010, providing safe and reliable transportation solutions in Rohtak and beyond.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rohtak Taxi Service began with a simple mission: to provide reliable and comfortable transportation services to the people of Rohtak. What started with just two taxis has now grown into a fleet of modern vehicles serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Over the years, we have maintained our commitment to safety, punctuality, and customer satisfaction, making us one of the most trusted names in the transportation industry in Rohtak.
